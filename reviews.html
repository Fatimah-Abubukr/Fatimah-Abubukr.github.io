Frequently asked Questions (FAQs):

How long do orders take usually?

If orders are via email, they can take up to 7 business days and if they are on call, they will be urgent and a maximum of up to two days

Do we work with other businesses to develop our products?

Sweet Delights is an independent business which started as a small one but has grown over the years with the incredible bakers that have joined and the mass population who have really enjoyed our products

Do you cater for specific dietary requirements?

At Sweet Delights, we believe everyone should be able to eat and try our lovely cakes so we try our best to cater for most dietary requirements such as being halal, some vegan, alcohol free, gluten free and even vegeterian.
